Relevant variables are at T->ev.*

scattered electron
=================
ep - momentum
th - scattering angle
ph - azimuthal angle
epx, epy, epz - x,y,z components of the momentum

Proton in rest for the proton case or the first proton for the neutron case
=============================================================================
p1p - momentum
p1th - scattering angle
p1ph - azimuthal angle
p1px, p1py, p1pz - x,y,z components of the momentum

Neutron
=======
np - momentum
nth - scattering angle
nph - azimuthal angle
npx, npy, npz - x,y,z components of the momentum

these varibles are 0.0 for the proton case

Recoiling proton for the proton case or the second proton for the neutron case
==============================================================================
p2p - momentum
p2th - scattering angle
p2ph - azimuthal angle
p2px, p2py, p2pz - x,y,z components of the momentum

pion 
====
pip - momentum
pith - scattering angle
piph - azimuthal angle
pipx, p1py, p1pz - x,y,z components of the momentum

Pt - Transverse momentum of the recoil proton
